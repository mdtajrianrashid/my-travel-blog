About Jaflong


Jaflong, nestled in the northeastern part of Sylhet, is a breathtaking destination where nature unfolds its charm in every direction. As you arrive, you're greeted by the serene flow of the Piain River, with its crystal-clear waters revealing layers of colorful stones beneath. The majestic Khasi hills, just across the border in India, create a stunning backdrop that makes the whole landscape look like a painting. On the riverbank, you’ll see local stone collectors at work—a unique and busy scene that adds to Jaflong’s character. You can also visit the zero point, right at the Bangladesh-India border, and enjoy a peaceful boat ride while soaking in the surrounding beauty. Not far away lies the tea gardens, rolling green hills lined with tea bushes, and the quiet Khasia tribal villages, where traditional lifestyles continue to this day. The air is fresh, the views are unforgettable, and every corner of Jaflong feels like a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.
